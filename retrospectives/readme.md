# Retrospectives
Other accurate names sometimes used in professional and productivity contexts:

1. **Weekly Retrospective** – Common in engineering, DevOps, and Agile practice; focuses on reviewing the week, identifying patterns, and improving workflows.

2. **Weekly Review** – Popular in productivity systems like GTD; involves checking notes, tasks, progress, and next steps.

3. **Reflective Practice** – A broader term for structured reflection about work to improve performance.
